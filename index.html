AGAPE

In our organization, AGAPE we believe in giving without expecting.
We would also like to convince you to share and help other, especially the poor.
Through this simple way, we ensure that nobody has to suffer alone in our society. Loving without having to be loved back is the motto of our team and we believe you should apply it too in your daily lives.
